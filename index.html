Rent and Lend Physical Games With Your Community

GameLend is a peer-to-peer platform where gamers share physical video games, consoles, and accessories. Try before you buy, or earn from your collection.

Get Started View Marketplace

How It Works

Lend Your Games

List your physical games, consoles, and accessories for rent. Set your own terms, approve requests, and earn from items sitting idle on your shelf. Build your reputation as a trusted lender in the community.

Borrow What You Need

Browse available items, filter by platform and location, and request rentals from fellow gamers. Try new games before buying, access hardware you need temporarily, and save money while expanding your gaming horizons.
